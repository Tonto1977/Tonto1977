# Toni Salminen

Founder, Ziirto Oy. Building the operating system for internal logistics consulting.

I run a Finnish consulting firm that turns warehouse and assembly floor inefficiency into measurable euros. Methodology is WorkStudy360®, anchored in MOST time-motion analysis and a six-phase project model called PMH. We work with clients like AGCO, SISU-AUTO, Saab Finland, Meyer Turku, and RMC.

The work I publish here sits at the intersection of internal logistics, time study, and applied AI.

---

## What I am shipping

- **MOST Video Analyzer.** AI-assisted BasicMOST analysis from video. Domain-calibrated against reference analyses by a senior MOST practitioner. Current accuracy delta against the reference: +14 percent.
- **Ziirto PT.** Internal AI-augmented project tool. Generates completion lists and engineer recommendations, reviewed by Back Office before client delivery.
- **Tonibot.** A working co-pilot for logistics consultants. Production target three months out.
- **WorkStudy360® toolchain.** Standard time matrices, layout simulations, BO quality evaluation under the K1 to K5 framework.

Most of this lives in private repositories under client engagements. What appears publicly here is documentation, demos, and tooling I am comfortable sharing.
